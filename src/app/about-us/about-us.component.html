Who We Are

The African travel Experts, we are!

Gilead Summit Holidays is your gateway to the enchanting wilderness of Uganda and Rwanda. Established in 2021, we are a tour company committed to providing affordable luxury services to our clients who wish to explore the natural beauty and rich cultural heritage of East Africa. We offer a range of safari packages to suit every taste and budget. Whether you’re seeking an adventure-packed journey through the wilderness or a relaxing getaway with your loved one, we’ve got you covered. Our specialized packages include gorilla tracking safaris, birding safaris, adventure safaris, honeymoon packages, fly-in safaris, and custom-made safaris.

For those seeking a more romantic getaway, our honeymoon packages are tailored to offer the ultimate romantic experience. From idyllic beaches to stunning landscapes, we’ll take you on an unforgettable journey filled with intimate moments that you’ll cherish for a lifetime.

At Gilead Summit Holidays, we pride ourselves on providing top-notch services to our clients. Our team of experienced guides and travel experts are passionate about sharing their love for Africa with you. We ensure that you have a memorable and safe experience during your trip to Uganda and Rwanda. From luxurious accommodation to comfortable transportation, we take care of everything, leaving you to enjoy your safari without any worries.
